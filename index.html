1

Each one teaches a different way real animators work — drawing, in-between'ing, and posing.

2

Use the toolbar on the side. Add frames, pick colors, hit ▶ Play and watch the magic!

3

Save your animation as an image, then text it to a friend — or print it as a flipbook!

Level up
